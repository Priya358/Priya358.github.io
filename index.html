Ariana Grande

Bio:

Ariana Grande is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She is a multi platinum, Grammy winner and she is known for her wide vocal range. She was born in Boca Raton, Florida, and is 26 years old.

Career:

Ariana has recieved a lot of popularity for her songs, but before she started singing, so also acted. At age 15, she was on the TV show Victorious and starred as Cat Valentine. She then followed up with the spinoff, Sam and Cat. After the show, Ariana started focusing her career towards singing. Some of her popular songs include:

Problem, Bang Bang, and Side to Side

Awards:

Ariana has won many awards due to her singing talent including:

Grammy for Best Pop Vocal Album

2016 Artist of the years

2018 Billboard Woman of the year
